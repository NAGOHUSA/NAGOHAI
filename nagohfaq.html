What is NAGOH AI?

NAGOH AI is your personal AI business assistant designed specifically for small business owners. It helps you:

  • Write professional marketing copy and social media posts
  • Create customer service replies and business communications
  • Generate product descriptions and service listings
  • Brainstorm business ideas and strategies
  • Save time on day-to-day business writing tasks

Think of NAGOH AI as a smart coworker who's available 24/7 to help you run your business better.

NAGOH AI is perfect for:

  • Etsy sellers — Create product listings and social posts
  • Real estate agents — Write property descriptions and client emails
  • Service businesses — Coffee shops, salons, landscapers, plumbers, etc.
  • Freelancers & consultants — Draft proposals and client updates
  • Any small business owner — General marketing and communication help

If you're a small business owner who spends time writing emails, posts, and marketing copy, NAGOH AI is for you.

Troubleshooting

Try these tips to get better responses:

  • Be more specific — Include details about your product, audience, or goal
  • Use click suggestions — After a response, click buttons like "Make it shorter" or "Give me another version"
  • Change the tone — Try a different Tone/Mode in the right sidebar
  • Ask follow-up questions — Refine your request in the next message
  • Try the Reasoner model — For complex requests, use NagohAI Reasoner for deeper thinking

Check the status indicator at the top-right of the app:

  • Green dot "Connected" — Everything is working ✓
  • Yellow dot "Connecting" — Wait a moment, the app is starting up
  • Red dot "Offline" — Check your internet connection

If you see "Offline":

  1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Try again in a few moments

If you get signed out:

  1. Click "Sign in with Google" on the login page
  2. Use the same Google account you originally signed up with
  3. Your chat history and tokens will be right where you left them

Your data is always saved. Sign out anytime by clicking "Sign out" in the top-right button.

A few reasons responses might be slow:

  • Using "Reasoner" model — This is slower but more thoughtful. Switch to "Chat" for speed.
  • Complex request — Detailed questions take longer to answer
  • Server load — During peak times, responses can be slower
  • Internet connection — Slow WiFi or mobile data affects speed

If responses are consistently slow, try switching to the "NagohAI Chat (Fast)" model.

If Google sign-in isn't working:

  1. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  2. Clear your browser cookies and cache
  3. Disable any ad blockers or privacy extensions temporarily
  4. Make sure cookies are enabled in your browser
  5. Try signing in on a different device

As a workaround, you can click "Continue as Guest" to try the app without signing in.
